About Chananel Vet Clinic

Chananel Vet Clinic Animal Hospital is a leading veterinary facility in Ghana, committed to providing exceptional medical care for pets. Our team of highly skilled and compassionate veterinarians and support staff are dedicated to the health and well-being of your animal companions.

We believe in a proactive approach to pet health, emphasizing preventive care and client education. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern technology to handle a wide range of medical and surgical needs.
